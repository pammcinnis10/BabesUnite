Tale as Old as 2011

Upon completing Chiropractic College, Brock decided a Californian vacation, visiting Adam who was living beach side, superseded getting a lizard pet. On a Wednesday in July of 2011, Brock and Adam walked into Hennessey's at the end of Hermosa Pier and naturally chose the bar with the blonde girls who were making bracelets.

Laura was fascinated to meet a couple of Canadians. At the time her knowledge of Canada included merely that Southpark chose to “Blame Canada” for almost everything. Brock and Laura discussed the comparison of ESPN to TSN as well as American vs Canadian driver's licenses. This was later determined a key move by Brock so as to retrieve Laura's full name before she was quickly whisked from the bar by her friends, abruptly ending the pleasant conversation with the newfound Canadian.

The following day when debating sending her a friend request on Facebook, Adam encouraged Brock along the sentiment of “what's there to lose?” Two nights after their initial meeting Laura met the guys and was introduced to the iconic beverage, a Canadian Hand Grenade. Thus began ECV1, Epic Californian Vacation, the 1st. The following two weekends were as described, quite epic; a whirlwind of beach bonfires, dancing, a Dodgers game, an impounded car, sushi, and more hand grenades.

Brock returned to Toronto, and being the casual cats that they were, they emailed daily and did not discuss the cross country obstacle to the romance they now found themselves in.

Laura flew to Toronto and Brock back to California, thus ECV2 & 3 ensued in equal epic proportion. Jokes about Laura quitting her job and moving to Toronto quickly turned into reality. On January 1st, 2012 she purchased the One Way flight to Toronto.

cLaura was welcomed with open arms, meeting wonderful people who would soon become close friends, and now wedding party. Brock and Laura were fortunate to first live with John, in his house in Pickering.

Temporary work permits became Permanent Residency. The couple became The Pack, with the addition of Snickers in 2015. In February 2017 they toured 5 countries in Europe. On a nice grass patch alongside the Reuss river in Lucerne, Switzerland, Brock took a knee and asked Laura, “You want to marry me, Babe?

Now we cannot wait to celebrate with all you!!
